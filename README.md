- MODEC Construction Steel Project

### Level 2
- Milestones
- Structural Steel
- Beams

### Level 3
- Shop Activities
  - Engineering
  - Detailing
  - Review & Approval
  - Fabrication
- Field Activities
  - Shipping
  - Site Delivery
  - Installation
  - Final Inspection
## Schedule Logic

- Engineering precedes detailing and approval
- Fabrication starts only after shop release
- Shipping permit required before delivery
- Site installation follows material delivery
- Final inspection marks completion milestone

Logic Types Used:
- Finish-to-Start (FS)
- Start-to-Start (SS) where applicable
## Scheduler Insights

- Proper WBS organization improves reporting clarity
- Separating Shop and Field activities reduces coordination risks
- Milestone tracking improves management visibility
- Early engineering delays directly impact fabrication start
